How to Join a Club in Carlow

A simple step-by-step guide for international students joining from abroad.

🔎
Browse & Choose a Club
Use this guide to explore all available activities. Tap any activity to see the clubs nearby. Use the age filter to find clubs suitable for your age group.
📝
Pre-Register Online
Fill in our Pre-Arrival Registration Form before you travel to Ireland. We will connect you with the right club and send you everything you need to know.
📧
Get Confirmation Email
After registering, you'll receive a confirmation email with details about your chosen club, contact info, and what to expect on your first visit.
🏃
Attend Your First Session
On your first visit, arrive a few minutes early. Wear comfortable clothes if it is an active club. Irish clubs are very friendly and welcoming to newcomers.
🎉
Become a Member
Most clubs will ask you to fill a short membership form. Many clubs are free or low cost. National body clubs may have an annual fee — usually €20–€60 per year.
📌 Important Note for International Students
You do NOT need to speak Irish (Gaeilge) to join any club. English is used in all club activities. Most clubs welcome new members at any time of year.
