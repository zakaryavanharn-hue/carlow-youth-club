Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from international students about joining clubs in Carlow.

No — English is the language used in all clubs and activities in Carlow. You do not need any Irish language skills. A basic level of English will help, but coaches are patient and very welcoming.
Many clubs are free or very low cost. Some sports clubs charge €20–€80 per year. Always enquire — it is always free to ask.
Yes! Most clubs accept new members throughout the year. You do not need to wait for a new season or term.
For sports: comfortable athletic clothes, trainers, and water. For arts or music: just yourself. Always arrive a few minutes early.
Carlow Town is compact and most clubs are reachable by bus, bicycle, or on foot. The Club Map shows each location so you can plan your journey.
Most clubs welcome young people from ages 5 to 26. Some clubs start as young as age 4 (like gymnastics and dance). Use the age filter on the home page to find clubs for your age group.
For your first session, just wear comfortable clothes. Most clubs provide equipment for beginners. If you join, some clubs include a jersey in the membership fee. Check the Club Profiles for details on each club.
Fill out the Pre-Arrival Registration Form. It takes less than 2 minutes and does not commit you to anything — it simply helps us prepare for your arrival.
